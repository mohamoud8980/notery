WHO WE ARE

“Offer quality and reliable professional legal services with adherence to ethics and transparency.”

Katib law office is a company law firm based in Mogadishu, Somalia providing innovative solutions to clients’ complex legal and business issues across different practice areas. We provide clients with the highest quality legal advice and representation in the practice areas of Finance,Corporate & Commercial transactions and Policy & Legislative Drafting.

The Firm’s clients are evenly divided between African and internationalentities. They include industrial and commercial companies, financial institutions, private funds, governments, charitable institutions and individuals. The Firm also serves as out-sourced counsel providing “in-house” legal advisory.
