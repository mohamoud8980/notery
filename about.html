About Us

Katib law office is a company law firm based in Mogadishu, Somalia providing innovative solutions to clients’ complex legal and business issues across different practice areas. We provide clients with the highest quality legal advice and representation in the practice areas of Finance, Corporate & Commercial transactions and Policy & Legislative Drafting. The Firm’s clients are evenly divided between African and international entities. They include industrial and commercial companies, financial institutions, private funds, governments, charitable institutions and individuals. The Firm also serves as out-sourced counsel providing “in-house” legal advisory. Our chief aim is to exceed the expectations of our clients positively and help them tread all legal minefields in their different industries,specialties, areas of work and spheres of life. To meet this aim, we focus on knowing our clients, their business and legal environments, their needs, expectations, time constraints and goals.
